About Us

The Fake news detector is web-based software that classifies reliable and fake news. Maniz Shrestha designed and developed the software as his Senior Computer Science Capstone project at Earlham College. The algorithm to detect fake news uses machine learning to learn from network metadata and sentimental analysis of the text.
